01 / The thesis

A fast agent with a vague goal is only accelerated drift.

The first responsibility of an intelligent system is not to act. It is to determine what the action means, what must remain true, and what evidence would justify completion.

02 / One request, clarified

The specification is the first product.

Before code, Ouroboros produces something inspectable: an explicit contract between intention and execution.

Illustrative input / before interview

A plausible sentence with twelve hidden decisions.

request: "Build me a task CLI"

storage: unknown
commands: unknown
failure modes: unknown
acceptance: unknown

ambiguity: 0.19

Illustrative Seed / after interview

Intent becomes executable and testable.

goal: local task CLI
commands: add, list, done, remove
storage: SQLite, local-first
acceptance:
  - ✓ survives restart
  - ✓ non-zero error exits
  - ✓ deterministic listing

ambiguity: 0.15

04 / The burden of proof

“Done” is a claim. Ouroboros asks for evidence.
